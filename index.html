WHAT IS .ZENOPACK?
A self-contained game package — HTML, JS, CSS, and assets bundled into one portable file. Works on any platform that supports the format.
🔒
100% CLIENT-SIDE
Nothing is uploaded to any server. All conversion happens locally in your browser. Your game files never leave your device.
⚡
UNIVERSAL FORMAT
Any unblocker or gaming platform that implements the ZenoPack spec can load your .zenopack files — share games across platforms instantly.
